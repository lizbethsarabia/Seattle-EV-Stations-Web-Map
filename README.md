# Geog328-Final-Project
For our final project, we plan on creating a web map that will visualize the spatial distribution, accessibility, and equity of electric vehicle charging stations and gas stations across Seattle. With EV adaptation rapidly growing, equitable access to charging infrastructure has become an important environmental justice and urban planning issue alongside existing gas stations. Our target audience includes Seattle residents interested in EV ownership, city planners and policy makers focused on sustainable infrastructure, and researchers studying transportation equity and urban design. It can also benefit EV owners who want to locate nearby charging stations within their neighborhoods. Overall, our project aims to make spatial data about EV infrastructure more understandable and engaging for a general audience serving as a practical tool for decision-making and future planning.
